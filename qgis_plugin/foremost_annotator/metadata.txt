about=FOREMOST Annotator overlays a configurable N×N planning grid on any QGIS map. Users click or drag-paint cells as Habitat (HAB), Restorable Accessible (RA), or Non-Restorable (NR). A tree-planting cost model computes per-cell restoration costs from cell size, elevation, road distance, and water distance. Export produces NumPy .npy arrays (habitat, restorable, accessible, cost) compatible with the FOREMOST multi-objective restoration optimizer. Supports session save/load and GeoPackage export.
